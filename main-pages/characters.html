Characters

For Staterra to change and evolve, it requires help. Assisted by municipalities with populations that rival entire nations, the continent is able to push forward and continue developing. Relationships between the regions blossom and shift with countless intricacies, and new technologies, customs, and ideologies grow and fade as the residents settle more and more in where they choose to call home. Yet there are a select few who stand out, taking the ripples made by communities over time and turning them into waves that spread across the continent and reshape everything they touch. Those few remembered in history as bringers of a new era.

A new era has begun to fall on the land, and it demands players to usher it in. As set pieces fall into place and the new names of history rise to the ocassion, history demands they be remembered, how such influential figures came to change the world. These records ensure that each hero and villain is remembered, unspoiled by the bias of victory. Come and meet the icons that molded Staterra into its next era, learn what you can about each one and decide for yourself who to rally behind and who to stand against.

However, be warned. Records aren't infallible. History cannot preserve what is unknown, it is up to you to fill in the gaps and make your own judgements. Some of the characters you will meet have carefully kept crucial information to themselves, though if they've done well, you won't know anything is missing to begin with.

Alexander Woods

Alexander is a young and energetic adventurer hailing from Nurture. His upbringing is reflected in his generally friendly and trusting nature, quick to help any who need it on his travels and always keeping an eye out for how he can make his presence beneficial to the locals. Despite this, he is usually somewhat reserved, happy to help others but slow to make lasting friends. This slight dissonance with the quality of Nurture is part of the reason he left home in search of adventure to begin with. He had felt coddled by the excessive amount of care and concern for his wellbeing and decided that a trip around the continent would give him some room to breathe and an opportunity to discover what else the world might have in store.

However, the young adventurer hadn't left his home municipality empty-handed. Primarily, he is accompanied at all times by a suprisingly docile and playfully wolf with fur like clouds, whispy whites and deep grays. The wolf, Storm, has been his best friend since childhood, though it hasn't occured to Alexander that his canine companion had far outlived the usual expected lifespan. Besides his travel buddy, Alexander also brought with him the trademark of his region, a strong sense of empathy. This has led to a particular interest in magic, hoping that his travel will not only show him new realms, but expose him to more emotions and behaviors than just hospitality, in an effort to learn and potentially master new magical abilities with a heightened proficiency.

Soren Arlow

An enchanter of wind and as ever-shifting, Soren has travelled from Freedom on his own journey. Unlike Alex, he has departed not to discover new ways of seeing the world, but to find the purest form of freedom he couldn't possibly imagine. He hopes to meet others and learn what drives them, where their joy and comfort comes from in order to better understand freedom of all kinds, be it the body, spirit, or mind. He dreams of returning home with all kinds of stories in the hopes of sharing the feeling with the others, preaching his adventure to others as "The Free-est Man".

Soren's extreme sense of freedom has led to an incredible mastery in the magical schools of wind and air, capable of impressive feats in the eyes of even arcane scholars. He has been known to often travel short distances by hovering just over the ground, or evade danger by flinging himself through the air in quick gusts. Those who know him will attribute his greatest ability to how he is able to clear the skies in a small radius from wherever he finds himself; a signature of sorts he likes to use to be remembered.

Kira Woods

Everyone describes Kira as a people's person, always eager to please others and make any social encounter one to remember. Her upbringing in Nurture has made her a natural at reading people and adapting to best meet their standards, though this has also led to her developing an interest in seeing what she can get away with. She'll put on her charms and start pulling strings like it's some kind of a game, getting a kick out of anyone she can catch off guard, though this is never ill-intended.

As Alexander's older sister, she often adopts a care-taking role towards anyone close to her heart, saving them from her usual playful antics. She can be quick to worry but usually hides this behind a mask of sass, one that Alex will complain to you about plenty. With her younger brother gone, Nurture begins to seem much too consistent, and she soon sets off after him in the hopes of finding new reactions from fresh faces of all kinds. Unfortunately, she hadn't thought to ask Alexander what his plan was, and it may take her a bit longer to bump into him once again.

Sylvia "Syl" Nightingale

If ever there was a visionary that spoke on behalf of Staterra, it'd be Sylvia. While the diverse yet connected municipalities of Staterra have all grown side-by-side and come to understand and respect each other over centuries of interaction and exchange, Sylvia believes the continent is still in its rudimentary birth. She sees a future in which all of Staterra's "fragments", as she calls them, are united as one, inseperable and homogeneous realm. Every civilization has something to gain from another and the "weaknesses" of each municipality would be covered by the implementation of its complimentary region.

Sylvia has set out on a campaign across the municipalities, hoping to win the hearts of the population with her ideas for reform. She promotes balance and peace, implementing her suggestions for a stronger Staterra wherever she goes while entrusting her followers to uphold those changes as she moves to the next municipality.
