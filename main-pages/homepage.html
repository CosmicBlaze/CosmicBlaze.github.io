Welcome to the world of Staterra!

An ongoing project and basis for a future novel by ALejandro Araque

Born from a Dungeons and Dragons campaign that never saw the light of day due to the COVID pandemic, the world of Staterra has stayed alive in my notebooks and short fiction over the years, gaining interest from friends and developing into something much more complex than the initial brainstorm that took place in an after school club in 2019.
