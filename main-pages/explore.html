Characters

These are the players within the world of Staterra, the chess pieces moving at all times as the fate of the continent comes to pass. Here, you'll meet heroes and villains, guides and tricksters, all with their role to play as the plot unfolds. Each has their own unique traits and characteristics that fall into their own categories, all somehow connected to who they are from the way they dress to the core of their spirit. Have fun seeing who's around, make some friends, and look forward to future additions to the list!

Magic

All residents of Staterra have access to some form of magic, though some are able to wield this power more effectively than others. These arts manifest through the emotions and spirit of the user, reflecting their will and identity in elemental form. Generally, this is the spread of magic throughout the land based on region:

  • Chaos - Fire and Heat
  • Freedom - Wind and Air
  • Loss - Water and Ice
  • Nurture - Life and Flora
  • Order - Light and Technology
  • Restraint - Earth and Spirit

However, those with particularly strong souls can often tap in to other fountains of magic, even becoming masters in those not belonging to their home.

Soul of Adventure

While most Staterrans are content living in the region they are born and raised in, there are those who feel they don't quite fit the mold. They often set out of home when they feel prepared (or not), and seek adventure in hopes of discovering their ideologies and like-minded travelers. They tend not to spend their time in one place too long and find excitement in experiencing as much as possible. These adventurers are known as individuals with strong souls, and are welcomed wherever they travel, often finding respite in local establishments and part-time work to pay for any expenses.

Alignment

A common mnemonic to identify a character's motives, behaviors, and attitudes to the ever-shifting world around them. An individual's alignment determines whether they are inclined to offer their last bit of spare change to a starving beggar or pretend they have no coin in order to better serve themselves in the future. Alignments have two categorizations with the following attributes. A character can either be Good, Neutral, or Evil, which is self explanatory. Within these confines, a character can be Lawful (following some code or set of rules and standards), Neutral, or Chaotic (making decisions based on an internal criteria that can be unpredictable to an outsider).

Motive

What drives these characters, what makes them tick? Everyone has a goal in life, something they want to accomplish, a criteria to find fulfillment in oneself. This can tell a lot about a character and how they may act or respond to the world, as well as provide insight to their background and what led them on the path they've chosen.

The World

This is the playing field for our characters, the stage on which their story will be told. Geography, culture, ideology, all this will play a role in deciding the outcome of every conflict and encounter. Come travel and see the vastly different regions, perhaps you'll find one you may want to stay in and call home, decide for yourself which seems the most exciting and enjoy your stay. There's no end to what you may find.

Staterra

Staterra is a large, continent which has been inhabited by a thriving population. The people of Staterra have decided to split the continent within a circular border, creating a pie where each slice is a defined region. As different as the Geography itself can be, so are the inhabitants. From humans to elves, aquatic humanoids and roaming spirits, many call this continent their home.

Municipalities

The regions that make up Staterra are divided into different municipalities, areas of land that have their own way of self governing (or lack thereof). These municipalities cover large expanses of terrain that tend to follow particular geographical patterns or consistencies, including topology, flora and fauna, and anthropology. The municipalities of Staterra are Chaos, Freedom, Loss, Nurture, Order, and Restraint.

Balance

At the center of Staterra stands a daunting, locked tower that pierces the heavens surrounded by a strangely empty field, one where snow always covers the ground yet never feels cold, where the sun is never hidden yet a faint fog always lingers. This area is known as Balance, a place most dismiss as an abandoned relic of a divine past, shrouded in mystery with no way of being studied. But just maybe there's more to it than meets the eye.

Wilderness

A vast, seemingly endless region surrounding Staterra and its established municipalities. Wilderness stretches on beyond the horizon, completely unmapped and decorated with nothing but strange ruins, unknown artifacts, and strange, powerful and savage beasts found more regularly the further you stray from Staterra. No one knows the history of this region, and is often considered a forbidden land for even the most experienced adventurers.
